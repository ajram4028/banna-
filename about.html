OUR STORY

Built on Principles of Excellence

Founded in 2009, BANNA Construction emerged from a simple yet powerful vision — to redefine luxury construction and interior design in India.

What began as a small team of passionate architects and builders has evolved into a powerhouse of premium construction and design. Our founders recognized a gap in the market — a lack of companies that truly prioritized both architectural excellence and luxury craftsmanship.

Today, with over 500 completed projects, 120+ team members, and dozens of industry awards, BANNA Construction stands as a testament to what happens when passion meets precision, and vision meets execution.
